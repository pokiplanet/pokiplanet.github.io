Privacy Policy

At PokiPlanet, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information you provide us directly (e.g., your name, email address, etc.).
  • Information collected through the use of cookies and similar technologies (see our cookie policy for more details).
  • Information collected when you interact with our forms or make a purchase on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To process and respond to your inquiries or orders.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To communicate with you about your account, orders, or other important updates.
  • To personalize your experience on our website and serve relevant content and advertisements.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data, including using encryption protocols and secure servers to safeguard your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for certain features on our site, such as:

  • Formsubmit: We use Formsubmit to handle contact form submissions. Your data will be sent to us via email through this service. Formsubmit's privacy policy can be found here.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze site traffic and usage patterns.

5. Sharing Your Information

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or to comply with legal processes (e.g., subpoena, court order, etc.).
  • If we partner with trusted third parties to provide services that help operate our business.

6. Your Rights and Choices

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for any processing we do based on your consent (such as receiving marketing emails).

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@pokiplanet.com.

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device to track certain activities, such as remembering your preferences and analyzing site usage.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more details, please refer to our cookie policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the bottom will be updated accordingly.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: info@pokiplanet.com
