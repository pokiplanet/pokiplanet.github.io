This Cookie Policy explains how PokiPlanet uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By visiting our site, you agree to the use of cookies as outlined below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions or preferences over time.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our site:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of the website. Without these cookies, the site wouldn't work as intended.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use tools like Google Analytics to track user activity on the site, which helps us understand how visitors interact with our content and improve user experience.
  • External Website Links: Some links on our website lead to third-party sites, such as eBay and PSAcard.com. These sites may set cookies on your device when you visit them, but we have no control over their cookie policies.

3. How to Control Cookies

You can control how cookies are stored on your device by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete them, or notify you when a cookie is being set.

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our site may not work properly. You can learn more about how to manage cookies through the settings in your browser.

4. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website contains links to external websites such as eBay and PSAcard.com. These sites may also use cookies to collect information. We recommend reviewing their cookie and privacy policies before interacting with their content.
